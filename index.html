About Us

Established in 2010, Madhurm Ghee has swiftly risen to become a household name in the Indian market. With our manufacturing unit based in Haryana and our head office in Delhi, we ensure the highest quality of desi cow ghee reaches our customers. Our ghee is sourced directly from Haryana Vita Government, guaranteeing purity and authenticity.

We are proud to be a Government of India AGMARK certified brand, with ISO, HACCP, MACCP, and FSSAl registrations underscoring our commitment to quality and safety. Our hygienic plant ensures that every package of ghee is produced under stringent standards, providing our customers with the best product possible. Our ghee is available in Ikg and 15kg packages, catering to both household and commercial needs.

History of Madhurm Ghee

Madhurm Ghee was founded in 2010 with a vision to bring the purest form of desi cow ghee to Indian households. Over the years, we have built a reputation for uncompromised quality and authentic taste. Our journey began with a small manufacturing unit, and today, we have grown into a brand that is synonymous with trust and quality. Our focus has always been on maintaining the traditional methods of ghee production while incorporating modern hygienic practices. This blend of tradition and technology has helped us achieve a loyal customer base and a prominent place in the market.
